Summary

I am a research chemical engineer who specializes in computational modeling to accelerate materials development and discovery. My toolset includes quantum mechanical simulations, chemical informatics, machine learning, and most importantly, fundamental physics and chemistry.
Research interests: energy storage and conversion, heterogeneous catalysis, surface science, reaction mechanism, AI for materials discovery.
